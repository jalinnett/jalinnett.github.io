Where it all started:

Ever since being a little kid, I've been drawn to mechanical things. I'd always stare at objects like ceiling fans and try to understand how they worked. So it was only nature that I was drawn to cars. As I got older, got toys for gifts, played car games online, and saw cars in person, my obsession grew.

Moving on to present day, after saving up I finally purchesed my own "fun car" a 2015 mustang. As I drive the car more, not only do I improve my skills behind the wheel, I make connections with new people who share my passion. It's been a great time with this car and I can't wait for the future and the modifacations I plan to do to it!
